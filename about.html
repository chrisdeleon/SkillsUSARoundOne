About Us

Where It All Began

Chamonix opened as a small antiques store in rural Scituate, Rhode Island where Nick and Jess currently reside with their daughter Alice. The store was voted “Best Eclectic Antiques” in 2017 by Rhode Island Monthly Magazine After three years of operating a physical locaiton and selling at the Brimfield Antiques Market, Chamonix closed its physical location and shifted to an online only retail platform.

Our Mission

At Chamonix we want to help make your house nice. We aim to find and sell very cool things that have history and intrinsic value - whether it be a post modern Italian designed chair, a one hundred year old hand-woven tribal Persian rug or a Soviet-era propaganda poster

Our Selection

The selection is varied and always evolving with a current focus on Antique and Vintage Persian rugs, Contemporary Art and Posters, and Objects for the home. Items are sourced from all over the globe - from New England auction houses to European art and rug sources

Where We End Up

Goods from Chamonix have found homes in NYC restaurants The Flower Shop, Speedy Romeo and Relationships NYC as well as retailers Jayson Home, One Kings Lane, ABC Carpet & Home and Urban Cowboy Nashville.
