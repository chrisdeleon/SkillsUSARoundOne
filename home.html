OUR
STORY

Chamonix opened as a small antiques store in rural Scituate, Rhode Island where Nick and Jess currently reside with their daughter Alice.

The store was voted “Best Eclectic Antiques” in 2017 by Rhode Island Monthly Magazine.

After three years of operating a physical location and selling at the Brimfield Antiques Market, Chamonix closed its physical location and shifted to an online only retail platform.
